Our Story

Who is "The Hype Advisor"?

We are a team of streetwear enthusiasts who have been passionate about the world of hype for as long as we can remember. However, we realized that while our closets were getting fuller, our wallets were getting thinner. We wanted to find a way to fund our passion, and discovered reselling: buying certain items for the low, waiting for a calculated period of time, and selling them high.

Reselling allowed us to make crazy amounts of money and buy what we wanted, when we wanted. After learning the ins and outs of the resell market for streetwear, we want to bring that experience to you. We know that getting into reselling is very difficult, so we wanted to streamline the process. With our experience, data analysis, connections, and economic knowledge, we can help you profit.

Our Service

A clothing and sneaker-based hedgefund with no barriers to entry

We are a reselling/investing service for streetwear. You invest money into us, and we put your capital in the streetwear market for profit. Using our connections, extensive knowledge and 5+ years of experience, we will buy items that we believe to be “bullish”, they will increase in resale over time. When we make the profit, we’ll return it to you, minus a small commission fee. For our customers, we cover all losses with our own profits (although we don't except to lose money)!

Why Us

Our process, goals, and potential

Like in any global market, we use our knowledge, experience, and connections to buy certain streetwear pieces that we predict will increase in value. Our businees in particular takes a lot of users' money and accumulates it so each use can contribute no matter how much or little money they contribute. We leverage all of this to make money with less risk and more opportunity to turn a quick buck. This profit can go towards funding your next cop, or whatever suits your interest. Streetwear is not just a passion for all of us streetwear enthusiasts, but a living, breathing market where millions of dollars have the potential to be made!
