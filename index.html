Our Story

Who is "The Hype Advisor?"

We are a team of streetwear enthusiasts who have been passionate about the world of hype for as long as we can remember. However, streetwear is an expensive hobby: as our closets got fuller, our wallets got thinner. We needed to a way to fund our passion.

About five years ago, we discovered reselling: by buying certain items for the low, waiting for a calculated period of time, and selling them high, we are able to profit. With that money, we could finally buy whatever we wanted, whenever we wanted.

Our Service

A clothing and sneaker-based hedgefund with no barriers to entry.

We are an investing service for streetwear based around reselling. You invest money into us, and we put your capital in the streetwear market. Using our connections, extensive knowledge, and AI models and data analytics, we buy items that we predict to be bullish: they increase in resale over time.

When we sell the items, we transfer the selling price directly to your PayPal account, minus a small 15% commission fee. During the rare occurrence that the items depreciate, we cover 100% of the loss.
